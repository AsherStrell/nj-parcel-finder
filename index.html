NJ Absentee Owner Finder

Draw a rectangle on the map to list parcels where the owner is out of state and mailing address differs from the property address.

Filters OOS and address-difference filters are applied after county-backed records are loaded.

        Data source order: county-backed records (NJParcels attribute cache) are attempted for every parcel with a PIN first for owner, address, and assessment fields (including values and deed metadata), then NJOGIS is used as fallback when county payload is missing. Some address comparisons are exact-match only; normalized text may still produce false positives on formatting differences.
